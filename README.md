## About us

**MikeSoft Studio** is a software studio based in **Accra, Ghana**. We help businesses turn ideas into websites, online stores, dashboards, and custom tools — without unclear pricing or last-minute surprises.

We agree on scope, cost, and timeline **before** work starts, so you always know where your project stands.

### Vision

Make getting software built feel straightforward — with clear costs, timelines, and results agreed before anyone writes a line of code.

### Mission

Help you figure out exactly what you need, give you an honest price, and deliver something that actually helps your business grow — not just something that looks good on the surface.

## How we work

1. **Understand your goal** — We listen, clarify requirements, and put together a clear plan with an honest price.
2. **Build the essentials first** — We focus on must-have features so you can start seeing results sooner.
3. **Improve based on feedback** — We refine based on how real users interact with the product.
4. **Support you long-term** — Ongoing updates and help so everything keeps running smoothly.
